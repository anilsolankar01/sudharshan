Established in 1995, we've been committed to providing premium quality edible oils for nearly three decades.

  • Each of our oils undergoes stringent quality control measures to ensure purity, freshness, and nutritional value..
  • Our journey began with a vision to deliver the finest quality oils to households across the Sangli
  • Over the years, we have garnered trust and loyalty from our customers through our unwavering dedication to quality and excellence.

We offer a diverse range of edible oils, catering to various culinary needs and preferences.We take pride in our rich heritage and commitment to health-conscious consumers, offering oils that contribute to a balanced and wholesome diet.As a trusted name in the industry, we are honored to be a part of our customers' culinary experiences, enriching their meals with the goodness of nature.

Why Choose Sudharshan?

The Triad of Excellence: Pure, Taste, and Health Selecting our brand means choosing a trusted partner in your culinary journey, offering a wide array of premium oils tailored to your needs.
